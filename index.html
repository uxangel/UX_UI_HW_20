Department of Labor Website Redesign

Problem:
Americans who have been affected by the COVID-19 pandemic need to find paid-leave resources on the Department of Labor Website, but they struggle to navigate through the overwhelming load of information on the site. How might we help users have a better sense of direction in order to access the Families First Coronavirus Response Act: Employee Paid Leave Rights page in a timely manner.
My Role:
UX/UI Designer + Researcher
